FAQ

Do you have openings for Postdoctoral Researcher or visiting scholars?

Yes, please send me your CV.


I am interested in working with you for my Ph.D., what should I do?

Please apply to Harvard University and mention me in your application. Feel free to send me your CV and transcripts.


I am a undergraduate student or master student, can I join your lab?

Yes, we have openings for undergraduate and master students with **strong** motivations. However, we have the following requirements:

  • Have taken at least one course in computer systems, e.g., Operating Systems, Distributed Systems, Computer Networks, etc.
  • Able to commit at least 10 hours per week for six months

Before you email me, we have a screen test to make sure you have the necessary skills, please take one issue labeled with "help-wanted" from libCacheSim and solve it.

If you encounter any difficulty, please leave comments on GitHub. Getting familiar with the codebase is a good way to get started.


I am applying for graduate school, can you write a recommendation letter for me?

I am happy to support your application, however, I can only write strong letters for students with whom I have had 20+ hours of interactions, e.g., office hour, project discussions...


I am applying for a green card, can you write a recommendation letter for me?

I am only able to write letters if we have multiple interactions before.


I sent you an email but did not get a response, what should I do?

I read every email very carefully. However, due to the high volume of emails, I apologize for not being able to respond to every email.
