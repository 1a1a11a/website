Hi. I'm Juncheng Yang.

I am a second year Ph.D. student in the Computer Science Department at Carnegie Mellon University where I am fortunate to work with Rashmi Vinayak as part of the Parallel Data Lab. My research interests broadly include CDN caching, fault tolerance and load balancing. Previously, I worked with Ymir Vigfusson at Emory University on various storage caching related projects. I also go by Jason, so feel free to call me Jason :)
