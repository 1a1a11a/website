I have several hobbies in the life except talking :(.

Programming is the first one.
I began my programming career with Pascal in 2003 when I was in junior school. After that, I self-studied VB and C in high school. It was really a difficult decision when I had to decide my major in the university. I like computer and programming, I like chemistry as well. Finally considering the fact that I could programming at home, while I could not conduct chemical experiments at home, I chose chemistry as my major. Even though, I didn't give up programming due to my strong interests in it. I took discrete math and audited some C++ during my undergraduate life. Later even after I joined Emory chemistry PhD program, I still kept reading news about technology every day, watching most events of Google, Apple and Microsoft. And I felt like I was really very haapy when I read the new, watch the events and programming. Then I thought over and over, I realized that it might be the time for a change. Therefore, I quitted PhD program in chemistry and began my life in computer science.
